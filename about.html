Biography

I'm a Mumbai-based filmmaker with a focus on documentary, narrative short, and brand films. My work is drawn to ordinary moments that carry extraordinary weight — a hand gesture, the quality of light at 4pm, the pause before someone speaks.

Currently in my final year studying Information Technology at Somaiya Vidyavihar University, I approach filmmaking as a discipline of sustained attention. I shoot, direct, and often edit my own work — believing that the relationship between camera and subject should be developed by one mind, not fragmented across departments.

Open to documentary, narrative, and commercial collaborations that value slowness, precision, and intentionality.
